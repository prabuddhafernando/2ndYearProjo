About us More information about our product.

Route me app is a traffic visualizing app. As congestion isolates people from business activities because due to the traffic condition employees always arrive late for work this app will help them to avoid traffic. Therefore it creates a less productive work force who are frustrated and tired at the beginning of the day . They cannot work efficiently and effectively the rest of the day. And too often they are tensed when they get to work. Many people that think of when it comes to congested roadways is the delay. In the morning commute there is additional stress because delays caused by traffic can make people late for work. Another effect of traffic congestion related to delays is the inability to estimate travel times. Route me app will bring solution to all the problems discussed above.
