This is a community-based traffic visualizing app. It joins drivers in your area who share real-time traffic and road info to save time, money, and improves daily travelling for all. In here millions of drivers out on the roads, work together to outsmart traffic and get everyone the best route to work and back, every day. By conecting to one another, we help people to improve the quality of everyone's daily driving. That helps them to avoid the frustration of sitting in traffic and reduces stress.
